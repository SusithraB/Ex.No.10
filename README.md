# PROMPT ENGINEERING TECHNIQUES (DETAILED)


## 1. Query Decomposition Technique

This technique involves breaking a complex problem into smaller sub-questions.

Instead of asking:
“Explain climate change”

We ask:

* What are the causes?
* What are the impacts?
* What are the solutions?

Advantages:

* Better clarity
* Step-by-step understanding
* Improved structured output

---

## 2. Decision-Making Prompting

This technique forces AI to select between multiple options before generating content.

Example:
“Should the content be written for beginners or professionals?”

Advantages:

* Better audience targeting
* More relevant output
* Improved content personalization

---

## 3. Answer Engineering Technique

This technique specifies formatting rules for output.

Example:

* Use headings
* Use bullet points
* Maintain formal tone

Advantages:

* Structured presentation
* Easy readability
* Academic formatting

---

## 4. Semantic Filtering Technique

This technique restricts unnecessary or irrelevant content.

Example:
“Explain AI in healthcare without technical jargon.”

Advantages:

* Removes noise
* Improves focus
* Simplifies content

---

## 5. Hierarchical Prompting

Content is generated in layers:

* General introduction
* Detailed explanation
* Specific examples

Advantages:

* Logical flow
* Better organization
* Gradual understanding

---

## 6. Role-Based Prompting

The AI is assigned a professional role.

Example:
“Act as a climate scientist…”

Advantages:

* Domain-specific language
* Professional tone
* Realistic explanations

---

## 7. Iterative Refinement

The output is improved step-by-step using multiple prompts.

Stages:

* Initial output
* Feedback-based improvement
* Final polished version

Advantages:

* High accuracy
* Better alignment with requirements
* Improved quality

---

## 8. Tail Generation Technique

This extends content into future implications or predictions.

Advantages:

* Deep analysis
* Future insights
* Extended understanding

* Industrial pollution
* Excessive use of transportation systems


### Environmental Impacts

* Avoid plastic usage

Government Level:

* Promote renewable energy projects
